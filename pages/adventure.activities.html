Unforgettable Quad Biking Experiences

At Wild Rides Africa, we bring you closer to the beauty of Victoria Falls, Zimbabwe through exciting quad biking adventures. Feel the thrill of the ride while taking in breathtaking views and creating memories that will last a lifetime.

Why Choose Our Quad Biking Tours?

  • Friendly, professional guides with extensive local knowledge
  • Safe, easy-to-operate quad bikes suitable for all skill levels
  • Spectacular routes through diverse landscapes and local villages
  • Opportunities for wildlife spotting in their natural habitat
  • Perfect for families, friends, and solo adventurers alike

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your quad biking adventure.

No prior experience is necessary! Our guides provide a comprehensive briefing and basic training before every tour. Our quad bikes are easy to operate, and we'll make sure you're comfortable before heading out.

We recommend comfortable, weather-appropriate clothing that you don't mind getting a bit dusty. Closed shoes are essential. We provide helmets and safety gear. Sunglasses, sunscreen, and a camera are also good to bring along.

Children aged 12 and above can ride their own quad bikes on our beginner routes. Younger children (aged 6-11) can ride as passengers with an adult. Safety is our top priority, and all riders receive proper instruction and safety gear.

Depending on the route and season, you might spot various animals including elephants, buffaloes, impala and numerous bird species. Our guides are knowledgeable about local wildlife and will help you identify what you see.

Yes! We welcome groups of all sizes. For larger groups (more than 10 people), please contact us in advance so we can make special arrangements to accommodate everyone. We can customize experiences for corporate events, team building, or special celebrations.
